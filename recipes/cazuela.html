Cazuela

Description

This is a hearty stew from the mountains of Chile. It's a great way to stay warm in chilly weather.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of beef, short rib, or brisket cut into large pieces
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 potatoes
  • chopped cilantro
  • salt and pepper

    • Instructions

    1. Season and brown the meat, then set aside
    2. Boil potatoes in a large pot of water
    3. Add in vegetables and meat
    4. Serve with chopped cilantro on top
